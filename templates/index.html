Emerald Pea Pasta
Procedure: Place peas & mint in a food processor & puree. Add remaining ingredients & pulse until a ball of dough forms. Turn out onto a floured surface & knead until smooth & pliable like a firm bread dough. Place dough in a bowl, cover with plastic wrap & let rest for at least 30 minutes. Shape & cook dough as desired.

Gooey Brownies
Procedure: Prepare brownie batter according to package directions; stir in sour cream, chocolate morsels, and pecans. Spoon into a greased 13 x9 inch pan.

Polo Dosa
Procedure: HEAT a dosa plate or a non stick tawa. Put in a little oil or ghee and smear it well. Pour on 75 ml polo dosa batter. Spread it well. Cook till done. Similarly prepare three more polo dosas. Mix the grated coconut and jaggery. Serve the sweetened coconut with the polo dosa.
